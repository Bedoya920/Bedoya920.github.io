My Projects

Bubble Typing

Bubble Typing is a typing game developed during the Global Game Jam 2025, designed to help you take care of your hands while having fun. With simple mechanics and just a few minutes a day, you can significantly improve your health in front of the computer.

Al ritmo de las leyendas

Al ritmo de las leyendas is a 2D mobile music game where you step into the shoes of César, a boy from Amagá, Antioquia. His mission: to face the legends of the Southwestern Antioquia region using the power of the region's traditional music.

Rauzo

Rauzo is a project developed in collaboration with the Sustainability Office of the University of Medellín, aiming to represent the fauna, flora, and landscapes of this educational environment through a 2D runner video game.

Haunted Blind

Haunted Blind is a game designed for visually impaired players, where the goal is to escape from an abandoned place. The project aims to provide an immersive horror experience as players explore different maps, which will soon be created by the community.
